Hi there, I'm Yash Sanghvi!

I'm currently employed as a project scientist at IIT Madras under Prof. Uday K. I graduated from IIT Bombay with a dual degree in Electrical Engineering (B. Tech + M. Tech.) in 2018.

Currently, I am working on the inverse scattering problem, focussing on mitigating the false minima encountered due to the global non-linear effects. My research interests include computational imaging, inverse problems, vision and intersection of one more of these fields with compressed sensing and machine learning.
